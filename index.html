So why are you reading this?

Because you are convinced that starting a space in India for adults and kids to work alongside is important.

But, it's already there.

Actually, not really. There are many places which do hobbies for adults and a lot of maker spaces. However, what we want to do is combine all of that. We want to create a space that lets adults and kids of any and all ages mix and create and learn!

Below are the ones already out there DIY hacking ikpeden

What will you do!

Robots, Woodwork, glow in the dark unicorns! Take your pick! You make what you want. We will teach you the skills and assist you the entire way.

Audience

In India, while the education resources in design industry are great for the age group of 18 - 24. Beyond that however, we still don't have enough resources to encourage people to dabble in this industry. As we all surely realise at this point, design is an industry that circumvents all ages.

To tackle this, 'FilterKaapi' wishes to make design education available to people of all ages and communities with a special focus on age groups 8-16 and 45+.
